============================================================
18. BUSINESS RULES
============================================================

The SOP Domain enforces the following rules.

• Every SOP belongs to one Organization.

• Every SOP has one Owner.

• Every published SOP has at least one approved version.

• SOPs shall preserve complete version history.

• AI-generated SOPs require governance according to Organization policy.

• SOP relationships shall remain traceable.

• Published SOPs become immutable until superseded by a newer approved version.

============================================================
19. DOMAIN CONSTRAINTS
============================================================

SOP management shall enforce the following constraints.

Ownership

• SOP ownership is mandatory.

Approval

• Publication requires successful completion of the approval workflow.

Security

• SOPs inherit Organization and Project permissions.

Compliance

• Mandatory review cycles shall be enforced.

Audit

• All revisions and approvals shall be permanently auditable.

AI

• AI shall not automatically publish or modify approved SOPs.
