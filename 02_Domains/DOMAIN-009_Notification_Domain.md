DOCUMENT PURPOSE
============================================================

This document defines the Notification Domain of Project ATLAS.

The Notification Domain manages enterprise communication generated by platform events.

Notifications inform users about meetings, actions, approvals, AI recommendations, system events, workflow changes, and organizational activities.

The domain ensures timely, secure, configurable, and auditable delivery of information.

17. BUSINESS RULES
============================================================

The Notification Domain enforces the following rules.

• Every Notification belongs to one Organization.

• Every Notification references a business event.

• Delivery history shall remain permanently auditable.

• User preferences shall be respected unless overridden by Organization policy.

• Security notifications cannot be disabled.

• Duplicate notifications shall be minimized where possible.

• Expired notifications shall not be delivered.

============================================================
18. DOMAIN CONSTRAINTS
============================================================

Notification management shall enforce the following constraints.

Security

• Notifications inherit Organization permissions.

Privacy

• Notifications shall never expose unauthorized information.

Delivery

• Every delivery attempt shall be recorded.

Retention

• Notification history follows Organization retention policies.

Localization

• Notifications shall support multiple languages.

Audit

• Notification generation, routing, and delivery shall remain permanently auditable.
