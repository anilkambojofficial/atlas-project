# 1. Product Identity

Project ATLAS is an AI-native Enterprise Knowledge Platform delivered as a multi-tenant SaaS application.

Its purpose is to transform organizational conversations, meetings, documents, decisions, SOPs, and operational knowledge into structured, searchable, reusable intelligence.

ATLAS is not merely a meeting recorder. Meetings are one source of enterprise knowledge.

---

# 2. Vision

Create the world's most intelligent enterprise knowledge platform that preserves, connects, and continuously improves organizational knowledge.

---

# 3. Mission

Enable organizations to convert every important discussion into reusable business knowledge that improves execution, reduces knowledge loss, and accelerates decision-making.

---

# 4. Core Business Problem

Today, organizational knowledge is fragmented across:

- Meetings
- Emails
- WhatsApp
- Slack
- Microsoft Teams
- Zoom
- Google Meet
- Documents
- Human memory

This fragmentation causes:

- Repeated discussions
- Lost decisions
- Missed action items
- Inconsistent SOPs
- Employee dependency
- Knowledge loss when employees leave

ATLAS exists to solve these problems.
